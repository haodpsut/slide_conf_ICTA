Conclusion

Key Contribution

This study successfully demonstrates that a Residual CNN is highly effective for robust quishing detection on low-resolution (69x69) QR codes, achieving a state-of-the-art ROC-AUC of 0.9313.

Superior Performance

Residual CNN outperforms Vanilla CNN, XGBoost, and the existing baseline in both accuracy and stability.

Enhanced Robustness

The model maintains high performance under common image distortions, making it suitable for real-world deployment.
