Introduction: The Rise of "Quishing"

What is Quishing?

  • QR Code-based Phishing attacks.
  • Redirect users to fraudulent sites or trigger malware.
  • A growing and significant cybersecurity threat.

The Core Challenge

  • Mobile scans often produce low-resolution (e.g., 69x69 pixel) images.
  • Images are prone to distortions like blur and compression.
  • Baseline models falter under these real-world conditions.

Our Goal:

Develop a robust detection model for low-resolution QR codes that maintains high accuracy even with image perturbations.
