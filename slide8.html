Experimental Setup

Training Procedure

  • **Optimizer:** AdamW (lr=0.001, wd=10-5)
  • **Learning Rate Scheduler:** Cosine Annealing
  • **Regularization:** Dropout (0.3), Early Stopping (patience=5)
  • **Validation:** 10-fold Cross-Validation for robustness

Evaluation Metrics

  • **Primary:** ROC-AUC
  • **Secondary:** Accuracy (%), F1-Score

Robustness Testing

Models are evaluated against common real-world image perturbations:

**Gaussian Blur** (σ = 1-3)
**JPEG Compression** (quality = 20-50)
**Rotation** (±10°)

The goal is to ensure accuracy degradation (Δacc) is minimal (≤ 15%).
