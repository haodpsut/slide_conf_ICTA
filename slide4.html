Methodology Overview

1. Dataset

Utilize 69x69 QR dataset from Trad & Chehab [1]. Normalize and augment images.

2. Feature Extraction

Create a hybrid feature pipeline for XGBoost (Image-based, Metadata, URL-based).

3. Model Training

Train three models: Vanilla CNN, Residual CNN (on images), and XGBoost (on features).

4. Evaluation

Compare models on ROC-AUC, Accuracy, F1. Test robustness against perturbations.

The entire process is designed to find a model that is both accurate and resilient to real-world image degradation.
